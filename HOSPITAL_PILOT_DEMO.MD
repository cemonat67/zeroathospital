PILOT DEMO ZERO AT HOSPITAL IZMIR HASTANELERI  Overview of Selected Izmir Hospitals

Acıbadem Izmir (Kent) Hospital

General Introduction

Acıbadem Izmir Kent Hospital, originally established in 2004 as Kent Hospital, is the largest private hospital in Izmir . It became part of the Acıbadem Healthcare Group in 2023, making it Acıbadem’s 19th hospital in Turkey . Now one of the group’s flagship facilities, the hospital spans 89,000 m² after a major expansion in 2023 . It is a full-service, state-of-the-art hospital known for advanced medical technology and comprehensive care. For example, it features cutting-edge treatments like the Da Vinci Xi robotic surgery system, Gamma Knife radiosurgery, and a 3 Tesla MRI for high-end diagnostics  . The hospital is positioned as a top referral center in the Aegean region, supported by a highly experienced medical team and a patient-centered approach.

Location

Address: Ataşehir Mah., 8229/1 Sok. No:56, 35630 Çiğli – Izmir, Turkey . The hospital is located in the Çiğli district on the northern side of Izmir. Its campus is easily accessible via major roads and public transit (near the “Ata Sanayi” İZBAN rail station) and serves as a regional hub for patients from Izmir and surrounding Aegean provinces.

Capacity

Acıbadem Izmir Kent Hospital operates with a capacity of 272 beds and 12 modern operating theaters following the recent expansion . It houses multiple intensive care units, including a Level III general adult ICU and a neonatal intensive care unit, to support critical care needs  . The hospital’s indoor area more than doubled (from 40,000 m² to 89,000 m²) during the upgrade, allowing for spacious inpatient rooms and new clinical departments . As the third-largest hospital in the Acıbadem network, it is designed to deliver tertiary-level healthcare services across all major specialties .

Clinical Services

All major clinical departments are available, with some programs achieving center-of-excellence status. Notably, Acıbadem Kent has an Organ Transplant Center (liver, kidney, and bone marrow) and a dedicated Oncology Center, which were highlighted in the expansion as key service lines  . The hospital also provides 24/7 pediatric care with a neonatal ICU for newborns . It offers comprehensive cancer care – the Kent Oncology Center opened in 2019 and provides medical, surgical, and radiation oncology services, in collaboration with Houston Methodist Hospital for advanced cancer treatment  . Other prominent specialties include cardiology and cardiovascular surgery, neurology and neurosurgery, orthopedics, women’s health, and advanced imaging diagnostics. With its broad multi-disciplinary team, the hospital serves both adult and pediatric patients across  all branches of medicine. Academic partnerships are not a primary focus (as it is a private hospital rather than a university hospital), but the experienced physician staff includes many experts, and the hospital often participates in clinical networks within the Acıbadem group.

Digital Infrastructure

Acıbadem Izmir Kent Hospital employs a fully digital Hospital Information System as part of the Acıbadem Healthcare Group’s technology infrastructure. All patient records and imaging are managed electronically to support integrated care. The hospital is equipped with advanced digital diagnostics (such as digital MRI and CT imaging suites) and uses an Electronic Health Record (EHR) system that ensures data availability across departments. Patients can access services through online appointment scheduling and have access to an online portal for viewing test results. Data security and privacy are taken seriously – the Acıbadem group maintains robust info-security measures (aligned with ISO 27001 standards) to protect patient data . Furthermore, the facility’s “smart hospital” features include modern patient monitoring systems, and high-tech surgical theaters equipped for minimally invasive and robotic surgery. These digital solutions streamline workflows and enhance patient safety and comfort.

Sustainability

As a newly expanded hospital, Acıbadem Izmir Kent incorporates several sustainability and safety measures. Its building was renovated with modern, energy-efficient systems and materials (for example, improved insulation and HVAC systems) to reduce energy consumption, though specific certifications are not public. Acıbadem Kent emphasizes strict medical waste management and recycling in compliance with environmental regulations as part of the group’s policies. The Acıbadem network has a commitment to green practices; as evidence, Acıbadem has pursued sustainable design in some projects (their Maslak expansion and others achieved LEED certifications) . While a LEED rating for Izmir Kent Hospital isn’t explicitly noted, the Acıbadem Healthcare Group as a whole integrates sustainability into operations – the parent company IHH Healthcare was among the first in the region to publish sustainability reports and implement carbon footprint monitoring for its hospitals. Additionally, Kent Hospital’s expansion added more natural lighting and green spaces in the campus to improve environmental quality for patients and staff. Overall, the hospital continues to uphold high standards of safety (it adheres to JCI-accredited practices) and quality, which indirectly supports sustainable and efficient operation of the facility.

Related Projects and Innovations

Acıbadem Izmir Kent Hospital has been at the forefront of adopting innovative healthcare solutions. Following its acquisition and expansion, the hospital installed cutting-edge technologies like the da Vinci Xi robotic surgery system for minimally invasive surgeries, and Gamma Knife radiosurgery for precise, non-invasive treatment of brain lesions  . It also introduced a 3 Tesla MRI for advanced imaging diagnostics , enhancing its diagnostic capabilities. The hospital’s Organ Transplant and Oncology centers represent significant projects that differentiate it regionally . The oncology center, for instance, offers comprehensive cancer care and hosts multidisciplinary tumor boards for personalized treatment planning. In terms of service innovation, Acıbadem Kent now provides a fully integrated neonatal and pediatric intensive care service round the clock, which is a critical addition for the region . The hospital frequently participates in group-wide initiatives of Acıbadem, such as telemedicine consults and international patient programs. As part of Acıbadem’s global network, Izmir Kent Hospital contributes to medical innovation by taking part in clinical research and adopting best practices from sister institutions. Its recent major expansion completed in 2023 is itself an innovative project – doubling capacity and modernizing the facility to be a cutting-edge smart hospital, poised to serve more patients with high-quality care  .

Bazekol Health Group (Bazekol Çiğli Hospital)

General Introduction

Bazekol Health Group is a prominent private healthcare network in Izmir, formed from the legacy of Ekol Hospitals. Bazekol was officially established in 2023, when Ekol Health Group rebranded as Bazekol on its 20th anniversary . The group’s roots go back to the early 2000s: founded by ENT specialist Dr. Mehmet Baz, Ekol opened one of Turkey’s first private ENT specialty hospitals in Izmir (circa 2007)  . Today, Bazekol has grown into an international-standard hospital chain in Izmir  . Its flagship facility, Bazekol Çiğli Hospital, has been serving patients for over 15 years . What began as a boutique Ear-Nose-Throat hospital has evolved into a full-fledged general hospital, while still excelling in its original specialty. Bazekol Health Group now operates six healthcare facilities (including Bazekol Çiğli Hospital, Bazekol Sada Hospital, and several medical centers) across Izmir , delivering high-quality, patient-focused care. The group emphasizes “quality, trust, and patient satisfaction” as its core values . Bazekol’s hospitals are known for their experienced physician staff (100+ specialist doctors, including professors) and modern infrastructure . With its expansion and rebranding, Bazekol aims to be a leading innovative healthcare provider in the region, combining the expertise of the Baz family with Ekol’s two decades of medical experience.

Location

Bazekol Çiğli Hospital Address: Ataşehir Mahallesi 8019/16 Sokak No:4, Çiğli – Izmir, Turkey . This primary hospital is situated in the Çiğli district (northwest Izmir metropolitan area), in a central urban location easily reachable by local transportation. The Bazekol network also spans other parts of Izmir: Sada Hospital is located along the İzmir-Çanakkale highway in Menemen (north of the city)  , and there are medical centers in Balçova (southern Izmir) and Buca. The group’s distributed presence ensures accessibility; each facility is positioned with convenient road links and serves a broad catchment of local and international patients. Bazekol Çiğli, in particular, is near the Mavişehir area and Izmir’s northern suburbs, approximately 30–40 minutes drive from the city center.

Capacity

Bazekol Health Group is rapidly expanding its capacity to meet growing demand. The Bazekol Çiğli Hospital itself is a medium-sized private hospital; while exact current bed counts are not publicly listed, the group has announced major expansion plans. In 2022, Bazekol’s leadership (formerly Ekol) revealed a project to create a “North Ekol” medical complex with 500 beds, by integrating the newly acquired Sada Hospital and an additional hospital wing . This would make it one of the largest private hospitals in the region. As of mid-2020s, Bazekol’s combined facilities house several hundred beds across general wards and ICUs. For instance, Bazekol Sada Hospital (in Menemen) is being upgraded into a large campus with a new 35,000 m² building under construction to accommodate the 500-bed goal  . At Bazekol Çiğli Hospital, the capacity includes multiple operating theaters and intensive care units, supporting both routine and complex surgeries. (Previously, the Ekol ENT Hospital in Çiğli had specialized ICU facilities like a dedicated post-surgery ICU for ENT patients .) Today, Bazekol Çiğli functions as a general hospital with inpatient beds, ICU beds, and operating rooms sufficient for multi-disciplinary practice (the exact numbers are estimated on the order of 100+ beds and 5–6 ORs, based on its size and service scope). Additionally, the group operates an 18-bed Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation center and various outpatient clinics. Bazekol’s staff strength exceeds 1,500 healthcare professionals , which reflects its large operational capacity. (Note: Precise bed/OR counts for each Bazekol facility were not found in public sources; however, the planned 500-bed expansion underscores the group’s significant capacity growth.)

Clinical Services

Originally renowned for Otolaryngology (Ear-Nose-Throat) excellence (Ekol was Turkey’s first dedicated ENT hospital) , Bazekol now provides comprehensive clinical services across all major specialties. Bazekol Çiğli Hospital and its sister facilities offer specialized departments in General Surgery, Internal Medicine, Orthopedics & Traumatology, Cardiology, Cardiovascular Surgery, Urology, Neurosurgery, Pediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Ophthalmology, Dermatology, Psychiatry, Neurology, Pulmonology, Gastroenterology, Oncology, and more  . The group prides itself on multi-disciplinary care: experienced surgeons and physicians collaborate across fields to provide holistic treatment. Bazekol hosts specialized centers that set it apart, such as a Da Vinci Robotic Surgery program for minimally invasive procedures , a Cardiovascular Surgery & Angiography Center (with a team of 4 cardiac surgeons) , a Hand Microsurgery Center, a Spine Health Center, and an Obesity Surgery Center . It also maintains strong ENT and Eye Clinics (legacy strengths of Ekol), including advanced units for vertigo, tinnitus, and cochlear implants. The group’s hospitals are equipped for complex interventions: e.g., open-heart surgery, organ transplant (Bazekol Sada is being developed to offer oncological services, IVF, and cardiac surgery to northern Aegean communities ). Bazekol’s medical team includes over 100 expert doctors (professors, associate professors, specialists) covering all these branches . While not a university hospital, Bazekol collaborates with academia by employing many academic physicians and participating in medical conferences. Overall, Bazekol’s clinical profile ranges from routine outpatient care to high-end surgeries, making it a key private healthcare provider in Izmir.

Digital Infrastructure

Bazekol Health Group invests in modern digital infrastructure to enhance patient care and operational efficiency. All Bazekol hospitals use a centralized electronic health record (EHR) and Hospital Information System, allowing seamless sharing of patient data across the network. Patients can schedule appointments online and access their lab results through the group’s e-Results (E-Sonuç) portal . Bazekol prioritizes cutting-edge medical technology: for example, it has introduced the Da Vinci Robotic Surgery system to bring robotic precision to various surgical disciplines . In addition, Bazekol implemented an AI-powered Robotic Maneuver Chair (RMS) – an innovative digital-medical device for diagnosing and treating balance disorders (like BPPV vertigo) with high precision  . This smart robotic chair uses artificial intelligence and integrated sensors to perform vestibular tests and therapeutic maneuvers more accurately than traditional methods, improving outcomes for patients with vertigo  . The group also uses telemedicine tools; Bazekol’s website indicates that virtual doctor consultations are available for international patients and those unable to visit in person (with remote evaluation of test results and e-prescriptions). Information security and privacy are ensured by adherence to standards – Bazekol is certified in ISO 27001 Information Security Management, indicating robust data protection measures . Furthermore, the hospitals incorporate “smart hospital” features such as digital operating room integration, advanced imaging systems (e.g., digital PACS for radiology), and AI decision support in certain clinics. These digital solutions align with Bazekol’s vision of innovative, patient-centered healthcare.

Sustainability

Bazekol Health Group demonstrates a commitment to sustainability primarily through quality standards and operational policies. The group’s facilities are ISO 9001:2015 certified for Quality Management and ISO 14001:2015 certified for Environmental Management, as well as ISO 45001 for Occupational Health & Safety . By implementing ISO 14001, Bazekol ensures that energy use, water consumption, and waste disposal are managed in an environmentally responsible way across its hospitals. For instance, medical waste is rigorously separated and handled by licensed disposal services, and initiatives are in place to reduce single-use plastics in daily operations. Bazekol’s new buildings (such as the expanded Sada campus) are being constructed with modern, energy-efficient systems and aim to incorporate eco-friendly design elements (LED lighting, efficient HVAC, etc.). While specific green building certifications (like LEED) are not mentioned, Bazekol’s leadership emphasizes sustainable growth – reinvesting healthcare revenues into new health infrastructure and technology for the community  . The group’s focus on patient satisfaction is formalized by ISO 10002:2018 Customer Satisfaction Management systems , which indirectly supports sustainability by improving service quality and reducing inefficiencies. Moreover, Bazekol contributes to social sustainability by providing healthcare access to a broad population (including partnerships to accept public insurance – SGK – and serving medical tourists). As a locally grown institution, Bazekol is mindful of community well-being: it conducts public health seminars and runs patient education programs (e.g., pregnancy school, obesity and diabetes counseling)  . These efforts, along with a proactive stance on technology and safety, ensure Bazekol’s hospitals operate responsibly and sustainably.

Related Projects and Innovations

Bazekol has undertaken numerous projects and innovations that set it apart in the healthcare sector. A hallmark is the integration of advanced robotic technologies into clinical practice: the adoption of the Da Vinci Robotic Surgical System enables complex surgeries (in fields like urology, gynecology, general surgery, cardiac surgery) to be performed with greater precision and faster recovery . This positions Bazekol as a regional leader in robotic surgery. Another innovative project is the deployment of the AI-driven Robotic Maneuver Chair (RMS) for vertigo treatment – a first-of-its-kind solution in the area that revolutionizes how balance disorders are managed  . Bazekol is also expanding its infrastructure ambitiously: the North Izmir Hospital Project (mentioned above) is a huge initiative to create a 500-bed medical complex by upgrading Sada Hospital, including a new building and even an attached elderly care center for long-term care and international patient lodging  . This project, once completed, will significantly increase capacity for oncology services, IVF treatments, and cardiovascular surgery in the region . In terms of medical programs, Bazekol has opened specialized centers: e.g., a Heart and Vascular Center (with a new angio suite) , a Hand Microsurgery Center, a Spine Health Center, and an Advanced IVF Center, each bringing together multi-disciplinary experts and new equipment to provide cutting-edge care  . The group is also active in medical tourism and international collaborations – Bazekol markets itself as “Izmir’s International Hospitals Chain”  and regularly treats foreign patients, leveraging an International Patient Office that provides multilingual support. Educational innovation is present too: Bazekol runs community education programs like “Gebe Okulu” (Pregnancy School) for expectant mothers , and preventative health initiatives (e.g., free screenings and seminars). Finally, Bazekol’s core innovation ethos is reflected in its motto of combining “scientific expertise with humane values”  – it continuously updates medical practices (often in partnership with global centers) and embraces new healthcare technologies (such as AI diagnostics and telehealth) to improve patient outcomes in the evolving landscape of healthcare.

Medical Park Izmir Hospital

General Introduction

Medical Park Izmir Hospital is a leading private hospital in Izmir, operating under the nationwide Medical Park brand (MLP Care group). The facility originally opened in 2012 and later underwent major restructuring in 2024 when it fully joined the Medical Park network . It is now one of the flagship healthcare institutions in the Aegean region for Medical Park, known for its patient-oriented service and internationally accredited quality standards. Medical Park Izmir offers a “university hospital” level of care despite being a private entity – it adheres to Joint Commission International (JCI) accreditation protocols for patient safety and quality management . The hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and a multidisciplinary team of specialists, providing world-class healthcare to local and international patients  . Historically, the hospital was affiliated with Izmir University of Economics (IUE) as a teaching hospital (previously called IUE Medical Park Hospital), which underscores its role in medical education and advanced care  . Today, after license transfers in 2024, it continues as Medical Park Izmir Hospital with updated facilities and the backing of one of Turkey’s largest hospital groups  . With its modern infrastructure and comprehensive services, Medical Park Izmir is positioned as a reference center for complex treatments and an important hub for health tourism in Izmir.

Location

Address: Gaziosmanpaşa Boulevard No: 5, Alsancak, Konak – Izmir, Turkey . The hospital is located in the heart of Izmir, in the Alsancak/Konak area, which is the central district of the city. This central location offers very easy access: it is on a main boulevard close to Izmir’s waterfront, within walking distance of public transportation nodes (bus, metro, and tram lines). The facility is approximately 25 minutes from Izmir Adnan Menderes International Airport by car , making it convenient for international patients. Being centrally situated, Medical Park Izmir is surrounded by various amenities and is well-positioned to serve patients from all over the city as well as those coming from neighboring provinces. The hospital building itself is a multi-story modern structure (about 12,500 m² of closed area) in a busy medical district of Konak, near other clinics and diagnostic centers, which further facilitates referrals and emergency access.

Capacity

Medical Park Izmir Hospital has a capacity of 136 beds and 6 operating theaters, as of its latest expansion  . Within this capacity, there are both adult intensive care units and neonatal intensive care units to handle critical patients of all ages . The hospital’s facility spans 12,500 square meters of indoor space, efficiently organized to include inpatient wards, ICUs, a modern emergency department, a full laboratory, and medical imaging units . Recent investor reports indicate the hospital is licensed for up to 143 beds , suggesting a slight increase or room for future expansion. The infrastructure supports advanced care: for example, there are separate intensive care areas for general adults, coronary care for cardiac patients, and a NICU for newborns. Medical Park Izmir also contains specialized units like endoscopy suites and intervention rooms. Outpatient capacity is significant as well – the hospital can serve thousands of patients monthly in its polyclinic departments. In sum, while not the largest in Izmir, Medical Park Izmir’s capacity is substantial for a private city-center hospital, allowing it to deliver tertiary care services. It is staffed by an extensive team of physicians and healthcare personnel, ensuring a high staff-to-patient ratio that aids in maintaining quality care standards.

Clinical Services

Medical Park Izmir Hospital offers comprehensive clinical services across virtually all medical specialties. As a general hospital, it houses departments for Cardiology and Cardiovascular Surgery, Pulmonology, General Surgery (including subspecialties like bariatric and oncologic surgery), Orthopedics and Traumatology, Urology, Gastroenterology, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Pediatrics (child health and pediatric surgery), Neurology, Endocrinology, Nephrology, Psychiatry, Dermatology, Ophthalmology (Eye), Ear-Nose-Throat (ENT), and more  . The hospital has a strong focus on oncology: it features a multidisciplinary Medical Oncology department and collaborates in Radiation Oncology services (with access to advanced radiotherapy equipment) . It hosts regular oncology council meetings where specialists from multiple fields (oncologists, surgeons, radiologists, pathologists) review cases together, enabling personalized cancer treatment plans . In addition, Medical Park Izmir provides specialized programs such as an IVF (In Vitro Fertilization) Center for assisted reproduction , a Hair Transplantation unit, and comprehensive check-up packages for preventive care  . The hospital’s approach is multidisciplinary: complex cases (e.g. heart surgery, organ transplants in coordination with Medical Park’s wider network) are managed by teams of experts. Academic collaboration is part of its heritage – it has served as a clinical training site for medical faculty students of Izmir University of Economics, giving it an academic hospital character . Furthermore, Medical Park Izmir upholds international quality standards (JCI), which ensures that clinical protocols, infection control, and patient safety measures are at a high level . Patients in Izmir and the surrounding Aegean region often choose Medical Park for its one-stop service range and reputation for quality care in fields like cardiology, oncology, and advanced surgery.

Digital Infrastructure

As part of the MLP Care group, Medical Park Izmir Hospital benefits from a robust digital infrastructure. The hospital employs a fully integrated Hospital Information System (HIS) that digitizes all patient records, imaging, and lab results, enabling efficient and error-reduced care. Patients can use Medical Park’s online platforms for services: an online appointment system and a mobile app allow scheduling and follow-up of appointments remotely . The hospital also has a dedicated International Patient Center with multilingual support, which utilizes digital communication tools (like online consultation requests and telemedicine follow-ups) to serve foreign patients . In terms of clinical technology, Medical Park Izmir is equipped with the latest diagnostic devices – for example, it has advanced MRI, CT, and PET-CT scanners that are digitally integrated (PACS) so that doctors can access images anywhere in the hospital . The operating theaters are modern and support digital laparoscopic and endoscopic systems for minimally invasive surgery. Moreover, the hospital follows JCI-aligned IT protocols for patient safety: this includes electronic medication ordering and administration (reducing prescription errors) and digital monitoring systems in intensive care. Data security is taken seriously at the group level; MLP Care was the first hospital group in Turkey to publish a comprehensive sustainability report that included data security and digital ethics commitments . The hospital also offers telehealth services in certain departments – for instance, during the COVID-19 pandemic it provided video consultation options for some specialties. Overall, Medical Park Izmir’s digital infrastructure ensures efficient healthcare delivery: from quick laboratory turnaround due to automated lab systems, to an SMS notification system that reminds patients of appointments or test results. These digital solutions enhance patient experience and align with the group’s innovation strategy of “smart healthcare.”

Sustainability

Medical Park Izmir Hospital and its parent company MLP Care emphasize sustainable and ethical practices in healthcare delivery. On the environmental front, Medical Park hospitals follow national regulations for energy efficiency and waste management. The Izmir hospital’s relatively new building (fully renovated by 2024) likely incorporates modern systems that conserve energy – e.g., intelligent climate control and LED lighting in patient areas to reduce power usage. The group actively manages its carbon footprint and has published sustainability reports detailing its initiatives (MLP Care’s Sustainability Report outlines goals such as reducing water and energy consumption per patient and increasing recycling rates) . In Izmir, the hospital participates in the city’s healthcare waste program, ensuring proper segregation and disposal of medical and hazardous waste to minimize environmental impact. For example, all infectious waste is handled by licensed contractors in compliance with the Ministry of Health’s guidelines, and the hospital uses digital record-keeping (paperless workflows where possible) to cut down on paper waste. The hospital’s sustainable healthy growth philosophy extends to community health as well – Medical Park Izmir engages in public health screenings and educational campaigns, aligning with the UN Sustainable Development Goal 3 (“Good Health and Well-Being”) initiatives . A notable collaboration is with academia: Medical Park Izmir signed a cooperation protocol with Izmir Institute of Technology (IZTECH) to conduct joint research and develop health projects , fostering innovation that can lead to more sustainable healthcare technologies and practices. Additionally, the hospital prioritizes employee well-being and training (an aspect of social sustainability), and it maintains ISO 9001 quality management certification and other standards that ensure continuous improvement in operations. While specific green building certifications (like LEED) for the facility are not mentioned, the group’s strategy is to meet international quality standards in all cities with fully equipped hospitals . In summary, Medical Park Izmir supports sustainability through efficient operations, community engagement, and being part of a pioneering hospital group in publishing sustainability metrics and striving for long-term health outcomes in society.

Related Projects and Innovations

Medical Park Izmir Hospital has been involved in various projects and innovative practices, often in coordination with the broader Medical Park network. One key development was its restructuring in 2024, which was a project to upgrade the hospital’s infrastructure and services to align with Medical Park’s high standards . This included facility renovations, adopting new medical technologies, and integrating the hospital fully into MLP Care’s system. On the innovation side, Medical Park Izmir stands out for its emphasis on health tourism and international services – it has a dedicated International Patient Department with multilingual staff (speaking English, Arabic, French, Russian, Georgian, etc.) to coordinate treatment and travel for foreign patients . This focus has made it a hub for medical tourism in Izmir, contributing to Turkey’s reputation in that sector. Academically, a notable project is the collaboration with Izmir University of Economics (IUE): Medical Park Izmir served as the teaching hospital for IUE’s Faculty of Medicine, where medical students train in a real-world environment with advanced equipment . This partnership (established in 2014) brought innovation in medical education, ensuring that the latest medical techniques and research inform patient care. Another innovative service at Medical Park Izmir is the use of multidisciplinary tumor boards and personalized medicine in oncology – the hospital convenes specialists across departments to tailor cancer treatment, an approach that improves outcomes and is at the forefront of cancer care . The hospital’s adoption of JCI standards itself is a quality innovation project; it continuously updates its clinical protocols, patient tracing systems, and safety checks (like electronic wristband ID systems and barcode medication administration) to meet these benchmarks. In technology, the hospital has kept pace by installing cutting-edge diagnostic tools, for example a PET-CT for nuclear medicine which enhances oncology diagnostics and was introduced to broaden their cancer services . Moreover, Medical Park Izmir engages in research and development projects through its cooperation with IZTECH – focusing on biomedical research, new treatment methods, and possibly healthcare technology development as indicated by the signed protocol for joint research . Lastly, the hospital frequently participates in public health projects in Izmir (like free check-up days or awareness campaigns for diseases such as diabetes and breast cancer), underlining its role in innovation not just technologically but also in community health improvement. All these initiatives position Medical Park Izmir as a dynamic institution that continually evolves, implementing new ideas to improve patient care and health system performance.

Tınaztepe Galen Hospital (Izmir Tınaztepe University Hospital)

General Introduction

Tınaztepe Galen Hospital is a modern private university hospital in Izmir, operating as part of the Tınaztepe Health Group. It opened its doors in 2019 as the fourth facility of the group  , and it serves as the teaching hospital for Izmir Tınaztepe University’s Faculty of Medicine . Located in Bayraklı (the new city center area of Izmir), Tınaztepe Galen is a fully equipped hospital featuring expert physicians across numerous specialties . The hospital was established with a “5-star hospital” concept, aiming to provide high-comfort, high-quality healthcare and to add significant capacity for advanced treatments in Izmir  . As a university hospital, it not only delivers patient care but also focuses on medical education and clinical research. Tınaztepe Galen Hospital quickly became known for its state-of-the-art oncology and radiology services – the founders identified a gap in private oncology care in Izmir and positioned this hospital to fill that need (with comprehensive cancer treatment capabilities) . The hospital is managed by Tınaztepe Health Group, which has over 20 years of healthcare experience in Izmir and also runs Tınaztepe Buca Hospital and Torbalı Hospital among others . Dr. Mehmet Bektur, the chairman of the group, has emphasized that Galen Hospital would be a “locomotive” for specialties like oncology, stroke care, and advanced diagnostics in the region  . In summary, Tınaztepe Galen is a cutting-edge institution blending clinical excellence with academic prowess – providing tertiary care, hosting medical training programs, and engaging in innovative healthcare projects.

Location

Address: Manavkuyu Mah. 250. Sokak No: 23, Bayraklı – Izmir, Turkey . Tınaztepe Galen Hospital is situated in Bayraklı, a fast-developing district often considered the new downtown of Izmir. The hospital is housed in a 9-floor building on a campus of about 15,000 m² . It stands near the “Bölge” metro station (Izmir Metro’s Region stop) and is easily accessible via the main Smyrna/Bornova highway, making it convenient for patients from across the city . The Bayraklı location places it close to the city’s new courthouse and commercial towers, as well as other private medical centers, creating a growing medical hub. Tınaztepe Galen’s position is strategic for serving northern and central Izmir, and it’s roughly a 10-15 minute drive from Alsancak (city center) and 30 minutes from the airport. Being part of a university, it also attracts patients from wider Aegean regions seeking specialized care. The hospital provides on-site parking and is well-connected to public transit, which is beneficial for both daily patient visits and emergency referrals from surrounding areas.

Capacity

Tınaztepe Galen Hospital was designed with a 100-bed capacity in total . This includes an extensive allocation for intensive care: the hospital has 12 Internal Medicine–Surgical ICU beds, 12 General Adult ICU beds, 6 Coronary ICU beds, and 31 Neonatal ICU (NICU) beds . Such a large NICU (31 incubators) makes it one of the largest private neonatal intensive care units in the region, indicating a strong focus on newborn and maternal care. In addition, the hospital is equipped with 10 observation (step-down) beds for short-term monitoring of patients, for instance in the emergency department . Tınaztepe Galen features 6 operating rooms (theaters) capable of handling major surgeries, and 3 delivery rooms for obstetric services . There is also an 18-bed Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation center within the hospital, enabling inpatient rehab care for post-surgical and stroke patients . The facility hosts advanced diagnostic and treatment units on-site: a Nuclear Medicine Center, Radiation Oncology Center, Interventional Radiology unit, comprehensive Imaging Center (with MRI, CT, etc.), a Chemotherapy unit, Endoscopy suite, a Central Sterilization unit, full-service laboratory, Eye diagnostic center, Blood Bank/Transfusion unit, Cardiology diagnostic unit, Neurology diagnostic unit, and even a Hearing (Audiology) diagnostic center  . Such infrastructure means the hospital can provide a wide array of services under one roof. Since opening, patient volumes have grown – the hospital was initially planned to handle about 25,000 outpatient visits per month in its clinics  . The capacity is supported by the group’s other branches (e.g., some patients might be referred to Buca or Torbalı hospitals if needed), but Galen serves as the central high-capacity hub for complex cases. As a university hospital, Tınaztepe Galen also has capacity for clinical training: it accommodates medical interns, residents, and researchers, which indirectly expands its service capacity by having a larger medical workforce on-site.

Clinical Services

Tınaztepe Galen Hospital delivers a full spectrum of clinical services, with 26 specialty departments represented in-house . Major departments include: Emergency Medicine (24/7 ER), Internal Medicine (General Medicine), Cardiology (with an integrated Cardiac Care ICU), Cardiovascular Surgery, Gastroenterology, Pulmonology (Chest Diseases), Neurology and Neurosurgery, General Surgery, Orthopedics and Traumatology, Pediatrics (and Pediatric subspecialties like Pediatric Neurology and Endocrinology)  , Obstetrics and Gynecology, Urology, Dermatology, Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery, ENT (Ear-Nose-Throat), Ophthalmology, Nephrology, Rheumatology, and others  . The hospital also hosts subspecialty units and centers that enhance its care offerings.
